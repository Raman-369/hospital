About Sabbarwal Hospital

Sabbarwal Hospital, located on Bilaspur Bye‑Pass Road in Jagadhri (Hafizpur), Yamuna Nagar, is a multi‑specialty healthcare facility with decades of experience. The hospital focuses on patient‑centred care and provides services across several specialties including cardiology, pulmonology, nephrology, gastroenterology and dermatology.
